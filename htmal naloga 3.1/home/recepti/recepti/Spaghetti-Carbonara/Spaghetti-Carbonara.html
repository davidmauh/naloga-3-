Chicken Tikka Masalaents

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or bacon
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve some pasta
    water. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
    Add garlic and cook briefly. Whisk eggs and parmesan in a bowl. Toss hot spaghetti with pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat, then mix in
    egg mixture, stirring quickly. Add pasta water if needed for a creamy texture. Season with salt, pepper, and top with parsley. 2. Chicken Tikka Masala
