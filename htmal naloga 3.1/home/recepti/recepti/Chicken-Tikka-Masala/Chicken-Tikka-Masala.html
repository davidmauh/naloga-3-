Chicken Tikka Masalaents

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 400g tomato puree
  • 1 cup cream or coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  • 1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and garam masala for at least 1 hour.
  • 2. Cook chicken until browned. Set aside.
  • 3. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger. Add tomato puree and simmer.
  • 4. Add chicken and cream. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • 5. Season to taste and serve with rice or naan.
